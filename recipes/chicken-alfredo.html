A simple main course!

This tasty looking treat can be all yours just follow the directions below!

First off you need a few ingredient's.

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 1 ½ cups butter, divided
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into cubes
  • 2 (16 ounce) containers whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Now that you have all of the ingredient's follow these steps in order.

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute chicken until no longer pink and juices run clear.
  3. n a large saucepan combine ricotta cheese, cream, salt, Parmesan cheese and remaining butter. Cook over medium heat until well combined, about 10 minutes. Stir in cooked fettuccine and chicken; cook until heated through.
